About Us

Visualization: using a combination of AR visualization as well as interactive dashboards, we seek to breakdown the tweets in the Voter Fraud 2020 dataset from Cornell Tech and reveal interesting facts about the spread of tweets, their sources and other insights. Our project focuses on looking the retweets made in the first week of November that concern the topic of voter/electoral fraud in the 2020 election. We have created a Dash app that visualizes the top retweets during this period as well as trends in their velocity (i.e how fast they spread), as well as whether users retweeting such content were still active as of December. Our dashboard also takes a look at the URLs spread throughout this period. We also have generated a social graph of tweets and retweets during this period using Unity AR, which allows us to see clusters of users exhibiting the same behaviors/interests.
